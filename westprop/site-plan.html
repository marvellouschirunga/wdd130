About West Prop

Established in 2007, West Property Company (WPC) is at the forefront of property development in Harare, Zimbabwe. WPC’s business model is premised around fulfilling the needs of customers through the role of master planner, financier and developer of appropriately designed, sustainably built residential and commercial properties within the Greater Harare Area.

WPC envisages the simultaneous development of multiple projects across the City, through the systemization and standardization of the property development process, together with the application of the latest technology and cost saving methodologies and the establishment and maintenance of relationships with service providers along the planning, development and funding value chain. WPC’s success has been driven by many satisfied customers who have purchased their properties through our approach to infrastructural development of prime land banks that have been acquired, planned, subdivided, marketed and sold.

Our Developments

To date well over 2,300 serviced, surveyed and titled residential stands have been developed comprising in essence brand new communities within Harare, with all the required municipal services. The majority of our client’s properties now accommodate substantial middle to high income homes and have all compiled with City of Harare municipal by-laws to the highest standard. We have implemented our strategic plan of creating real estate valued in excess of US$500million and have commenced with project planning and infrastructural development of our land banks. Super-structure development of both residential and commercial properties has begun and significant strides have been made with projects in our portfolio completed whilst others are currently under development.
